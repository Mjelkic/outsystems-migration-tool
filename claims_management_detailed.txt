CLAIMS MANAGEMENT APPLICATION – DETAILED FUNCTIONAL SPECIFICATION

1.  OVERVIEW The Claims Management Application is designed to manage the
    full lifecycle of claims, including claim creation, validation,
    processing, and closure. It supports management of Claimants and
    Subclaims, ensuring traceability, auditability, and scalability.

2.  CORE ENTITIES

2.1 Claim - ClaimId (AutoNumber, Primary Key) - ClaimNumber (Text,
Unique) - ClaimType (Text) - ClaimDate (Date) - IncidentDate (Date) -
Description (Text) - StatusId (FK to Status) - TotalAmount (Decimal) -
AssignedTo (UserId) - CreatedOn (DateTime) - CreatedBy (UserId) -
UpdatedOn (DateTime) - UpdatedBy (UserId)

2.2 Claimant - ClaimantId (AutoNumber, Primary Key) - FirstName (Text) -
LastName (Text) - DateOfBirth (Date) - IdentificationNumber (Text) -
Email (Text) - Phone (Text) - Address (Text) - ClaimantType (Text:
Individual/Company)

2.3 Subclaim - SubclaimId (AutoNumber, Primary Key) - ClaimId (FK) -
SubclaimType (Text) - Description (Text) - Amount (Decimal) - StatusId
(FK to Status) - AssignedTo (UserId)

2.4 Claim_Claimant (Join Table) - Id (AutoNumber) - ClaimId (FK) -
ClaimantId (FK)

2.5 Status (Static Entity) - StatusId - Name (Draft, Submitted, Under
Review, Approved, Rejected, Closed)

3.  RELATIONSHIPS

-   Claim (1) to Subclaim (N)
-   Claim (N) to Claimant (N) via Claim_Claimant
-   User (1) to Claim (N)
-   User (1) to Subclaim (N)

4.  BUSINESS RULES

-   A claim must have at least one claimant before submission
-   Claim must include Description and IncidentDate before submission
-   Subclaim Amount must be greater than zero
-   Claim TotalAmount = sum of Subclaim Amounts
-   Claim status updates based on subclaim statuses

5.  BUSINESS LOGIC

5.1 Create Claim - Initializes claim with Draft status - Generates
ClaimNumber - Stores audit fields

5.2 Submit Claim - Validates required fields - Ensures at least one
claimant exists - Changes status to Submitted

5.3 Assign Claim - Assigns claim to a handler - Updates AssignedTo field

5.4 Create Subclaim - Associates subclaim with claim - Sets default
status

5.5 Approve/Reject Subclaim - Updates subclaim status - Triggers
recalculation of claim total and status

5.6 Calculate Claim Total - Aggregates all subclaim amounts - Updates
Claim.TotalAmount

5.7 Update Claim Status - If all subclaims approved → Claim Approved -
If any rejected → Claim Partially Approved or Rejected

6.  UI STRUCTURE

6.1 Claim Screens - Claim List (filters: status, type, date) - Claim
Detail (tabs: Overview, Claimants, Subclaims, Documents) - Create/Edit
Claim

6.2 Claimant Screens - Claimant List - Claimant Detail - Create/Edit
Claimant

6.3 Subclaim Screens - Subclaim List (filtered by claim) - Subclaim
Detail - Create/Edit Subclaim

7.  ROLES & PERMISSIONS

-   Admin: Full access
-   Claims Handler: Manage claims and subclaims
-   Viewer: Read-only

8.  DOCUMENT MANAGEMENT

-   Upload documents to claims and subclaims
-   Metadata:
    -   FileName
    -   UploadDate
    -   UploadedBy

9.  AUDIT & LOGGING

-   Track all status changes
-   Store:
    -   ChangedBy
    -   ChangedOn
    -   OldValue
    -   NewValue

10. INTEGRATIONS (OPTIONAL)

-   Payment processing
-   Fraud detection APIs

11. FUTURE ENHANCEMENTS
